About BSP

The Bahujan Samaj Party stands for the social transformation and economic emancipation of the Bahujan Samaj, which comprises the Scheduled Castes (SC), Scheduled Tribes (ST), and Other Backward Castes (OBC). Inspired by the philosophy of Dr. B.R. Ambedkar, we work towards creating an egalitarian society.

Our Mission

To achieve social transformation and economic emancipation of the Bahujan Samaj through democratic means and constitutional methods.

Our Values

Equality, justice, brotherhood, and the constitutional principles laid down by Dr. B.R. Ambedkar guide our path forward.

Our Struggle

We continue the struggle for dignity, self-respect, and equal opportunities for all marginalized communities in India.

Vision & Leadership

Our vision is to build a society where every individual has equal opportunities regardless of their caste, creed, or background. We are committed to the ideals of Dr. B.R. Ambedkar and work towards implementing his vision of a just and equitable society.
